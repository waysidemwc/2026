# Sunday Tournament Format (Finals Day)

This document describes the tournament brackets and rules for Sunday, as captured from `templates/sunday.png`.

## Overview
The tournament is split into different formats based on the number of teams in each age group. 
- **8 Teams** 
- **6 Teams** 

## General Rules
- **Ranking:** Teams are ranked 1 to 8/6 after League ends Thursday.
- **Splits:** Teams are split based on rank into Cup/Plate/Shield.
- **Seeding:** Rank 1 plays Rank 4 as a reward for winning the Round Robin League.
- **Match Flow:** Semi final winners play each other. Semi final Runners Up play each other.
- **Timing:** Games are every 30 minutes: 20 minutes game + 10 minutes for extra time, penalties, break (subject to refinements based on age).
