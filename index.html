Tournament Logic & Parameters

Foundational Constraints

  • 14 pitches (or 13 Pitches + 1 spare)
  • 15 minutes per slot: 12 min match + 3 min gap
  • Session Timing: both Tue and Thu finish at 8:30 PM latest
  • Minimal Movement: Age groups are ring-fenced to specific dedicated pitches

Capacity Math

  • Total Time: 2 hours per night (8 core slots per night)
  • Pitch Capacity: 16 core matches
  • Tournament Cap (14 Pitches): 224 core match slots
